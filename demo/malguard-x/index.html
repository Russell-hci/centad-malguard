Distinct Product Idea

MalGuard is a robustness triage system, not just a classifier.

It shows whether a malware detector still deserves trust after an adversarial attack by combining attack simulation, robust recovery, and Grad-CAM attention-stability evidence.

1 Expose hidden failure

A clean-accurate detector collapses from 97.92% clean accuracy to 0.29% under PGD-20.

2 Recover with same footprint

Family-balanced adversarial training lifts PGD-20 macro F1 from 3.16% to 82.77%.

3 Check model focus

Grad-CAM evidence suggests the robust detector keeps more consistent attention under attack.
