Problem

Malware image classifiers can be highly accurate on clean samples but fail when an attacker deliberately perturbs the input.

Clean accuracy is not enough for cybersecurity.

Protocol

  • MalImg malware image dataset
  • 25 malware families
  • Duplicate-aware SHA-256 content split
  • FGSM and PGD raw pixel-space attacks
  • MobileNetV3 PGD adversarial training
