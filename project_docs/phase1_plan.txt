# PRIORITY ATTACKS

## 1. FGSM (Start Here)

FGSM is the easiest attack to understand.

Formula:

x_{adv}=x+\epsilon\cdot sign(\nabla_x J(\theta,x,y))

Conceptually:

* compute gradient,
* slightly perturb image,
* fool model.

---

# Best FGSM Resource

[Official PyTorch FGSM Tutorial](https://docs.pytorch.org/tutorials/beginner/fgsm_tutorial.html?utm_source=chatgpt.com) ([docs.pytorch.org][8])
